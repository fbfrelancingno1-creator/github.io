This Privacy Policy describes how Leisi Family handles information when using our private TikTok content management tool. We are committed to protecting privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws.

This tool collects only the minimum information required to function: TikTok access tokens (to authenticate with TikTok's API), TikTok account identifiers, and video files selected for upload. We do not collect personal data from third parties or TikTok viewers.

All collected information is used solely for the purpose of uploading content to TikTok on behalf of the Leisi Family account. We do not sell, share, or distribute any data to third parties.

This tool communicates exclusively with TikTok's official API (open.tiktokapis.com). TikTok's Privacy Policy governs how TikTok handles data on their platform. We encourage you to review TikTok's Privacy Policy at tiktok.com/privacy.

We retain access tokens only as long as necessary to maintain the TikTok connection. Tokens are deleted upon disconnection or expiry. No video files are retained after upload.
